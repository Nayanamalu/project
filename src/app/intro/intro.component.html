ABOUT CHATBOX

Chatbox was designed to help organizations have better conversations with their website visitors in the moments that matter most. We eliminated frustrating per operator costs and chat limits to ensure that your entire team can leverage the power of live chat without having to worry about skyrocketing costs as your team grows.

Simply sign up for Pure Chat, grab the snippet of code to install on your website (or use one of our handy plugins), customize your chat widget, and start chatting.
